How custom orders work

  1. 1
    You submit a request.
    Tell me what you’re thinking, your budget , and when you need it by. You can paste links to inspiration photos.
  2. 2
    I review and email you a quote.
    I’ll confirm details, share a price (including estimated shipping), and an estimated timeline.
  3. 3
    You approve & pay online.
    If you’re happy with the quote, you’ll get a secure payment link Paypal.

    I sew & ship.
    When your piece is finished, I send a quick photo and tracking number.
